Library

The CPP-IPR library is located in the main building of CPP-IPR. It was established to support the scientific and technical programs of the institute.

COLLECTIONS

Collection of the library includes books, journals, scientific magazines, thesis and newspapers. The collection of books in the library covers the following subject areas:

  • General Physics
  • Astronomy
  • Plasma Physics
  • Mathematics
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Electronics
WORKING HOURS
The library remains open from 9:00 am to 5:30 pm from Monday to Friday.
USERS
  • Employees of the institute
  • Research scholars
SERVICES

The library provides the following services:

  • Circulation service
  • Reading room service
  • Reprographic service (photocopy)
